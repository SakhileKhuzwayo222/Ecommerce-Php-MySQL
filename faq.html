Frequently Asked Questions

Order Related

How do I track my order?
Log into your account and go to the order history page. Click on the tracking number to track your order.
Can I change my order once it has been placed?
Yes, but only if the order has not yet been shipped. Contact customer service for assistance with this.
What if I receive a damaged product?
If you've received a damaged product, please contact our customer service immediately.

Payment Related

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, Paypal, and payment on delivery.
Is my payment information secure?
Yes, all payment information is processed through a secure, encrypted connection.

Account Related

How do I reset my password?
Go to the login page and click the "Forgot my password" link. Follow the prompts to reset your password.
How can I delete my account?
If you want to delete your account, please contact our customer service.
